Best of Quinoa Blurb

Quinoa - pronounced keen-wah - is a grain, but not just any grain. It is considered to be almost a complete food. It is very high in protein, full of vitamins, gluten and wheat free, cholesterol-free and usually organic. And it is simple delicious.

In the kitchen, quinoa has a huge range of uses and lends itself beautifully to so many dishes. It is simple to prepare, easy to digest and most enjoyable to eat. Best of Quinoa brings together a wonderful selection of easy to follow recipes for breakfast, lunch and dinner, as well as soups, salads and so much more; there is something for every palate.

This exciting collection of recipes is a must-have addition to every cook's repertoire.
